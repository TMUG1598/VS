Our story began at the heart of the Covid-19 pandemic in 2020. We realized that with the amount of people who were either losing their 9-5 jobs, or weren’t able to go to work due to the lack of public transportation, the opportunity for work was limited. We wanted to help people make the switch and have the freedom to work from their homes, with jobs that they actually wanted to do. 

With this, we came up with Virtual Switchr, a company that will provide you online courses that focuses on introducing the necessary skills that you will need to start your VA careers. 

We offer a wide-range of Virtual Assistant courses. From improving your spoken and written English language skills, improving your customer service skills, project management, and even the necessary tools and programs that you will need for you to become a valuable VA to a wide-array of clients out in the market. We even offer soft skills courses that will help you improve your time management, work ethics, and leadership skills, and more.

Our mission is simple, give you affordable but extremely valuable courses that will help you reach your professional goals and have the freedom to work at the comfort of your homes, or to kickstart your professional careers in large companies.
